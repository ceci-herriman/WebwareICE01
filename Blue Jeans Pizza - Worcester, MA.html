About Green Jeans Salads

Salads and Brew Is What We Do

Since 1980 Green Jeans has been a Worcester tradition. Our famous salads have been awarded Worcester’s Best Cruelty Free Salads multiple times. The roots of our recipe and hand-stretched plants go back to Boston’s North End. And the extensive menu means you’ll find something for everyone. Order online. We’d love to have you join us in our new dining room. Or have us deliver your meal green and delicious.
